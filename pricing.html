Frequently asked questions

How does the free trial work?

Start a 14-day free trial of our Pro plan instantly — no credit card required. You get full access to all Pro features including AI content matching, deal intelligence, and unlimited rooms. At the end of your trial, you can choose to subscribe or drop down to our free Starter plan.

What makes ShoDeck's AI different from other tools?

ShoDeck's AI is purpose-built for B2B sales. Unlike generic AI tools, our models are trained on sales engagement patterns and understand deal dynamics. The AI doesn't just summarize — it actively recommends content, scores deal health, identifies risks, and drafts follow-ups based on real buyer behavior.

Can I import my existing content?

Yes. ShoDeck supports bulk upload of PDFs, presentations, videos, and documents. Our AI automatically tags and categorizes your content, making it instantly searchable and ready for deal room inclusion.

I'm a BuyerDeck customer. What happens to my account?

Welcome! BuyerDeck is now ShoDeck. Your account, data, and rooms have been automatically migrated. You'll find everything exactly where you left it, plus all the new AI features. If you need help, reach out to hello@shodeck.com.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes. Annual plans save you 20% compared to monthly billing. Contact our sales team at hello@shodeck.com for annual pricing and volume discounts for larger teams.
