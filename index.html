How it works

Three steps to bring your teams together.

1

Open a Room

Create a branded deal room in seconds. Add your team members — AEs, SEs, executives — anyone on your side who should be part of this deal.

2

Invite the Buyer's Team

Share a single link with your buyer. They bring in their stakeholders — procurement, legal, technical evaluators, the executive sponsor. Everyone has a seat at the table.

3

Build Context Together

Share proposals, answer questions, post updates. Every interaction builds a shared history that keeps the deal moving — even when new people join midway.
