A decade of building for sales teams

From BuyerDeck to ShoDeck, we've spent 10+ years obsessing over one thing: helping sales teams close deals faster.

Why we exist

We started ShoDeck — originally BuyerDeck — in 2013 because we saw a fundamental problem in B2B sales: buyers were drowning in scattered emails, disconnected attachments, and generic follow-ups, while sales reps were flying blind without any insight into what their buyers actually cared about.

We believed there had to be a better way. A single, intelligent space where every deal could live — where buyers get a curated experience and sellers get the signals they need to close.

Over the past decade, we've evolved from a simple content-sharing tool into an AI-powered sales intelligence platform. But our mission hasn't changed: make every interaction between buyer and seller more valuable.

Our journey

2013

Founded as BuyerDeck

Launched with a simple idea: give buyers a better way to receive and review sales materials.

2016

1,000 deal rooms created

Reached our first major milestone as sales teams across the US adopted the platform.

2019

Real-time analytics launched

Introduced buyer engagement tracking, giving sales reps unprecedented visibility into deal activity.

2024

Rebranded to ShoDeck

New name, new identity, same mission. Reflected our evolution from content sharing to intelligent sales rooms.

2026

AI-native platform

Launched AI content matching, deal intelligence scoring, and smart follow-ups — making every rep's AI sales strategist.
